Alabama

Mentally incompetent not qualified to vote until restored.

ALA. CONST. art. VIII, § 177

(amended 1996).

 

Any person who is impaired by reason of mental illness, mental deficiency, physical illness or disability, physical

or mental infirmities accompanying advanced age, chronic use of drugs, chronic intoxication, or other cause (except minority) to the extent of lacking sufficient understanding or capacity to make or communicate responsible decisions. ALA. CODE § 262A-20(8)

 

The court may make orders only as necessary. ALA. CODE §§ 26-2A105(a), 26-2A-136(a).
